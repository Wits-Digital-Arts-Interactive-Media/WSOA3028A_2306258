I have more work that I’m proud of in terms of the UI/UX and overall game design. I got to showcase my ideas and my skills as a programmer and designer.

I have more projects I’ve worked on for game jams and during my studies but these two rank top 2.

Sushi Go!

I think this one might make you hungry.Do you think you can be the ultimate sushi chef? Sushi Go! Is an idle clicker game prototype which I worked. The aim of the game is to make as much sushi as you can to be dubbed the ultimate sushi chef.

Resource Management Game

This is a resource management game I designed in 2021. The aim of the game was to produce resources and obtain new resources to help grow your farm. The trading system was a bartering system. Players sold some of their resources to “buy” better ones.
