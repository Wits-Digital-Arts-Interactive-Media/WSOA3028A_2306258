My Farm: Resource Management Game

Do you have what it takes to run your own little farm?

This is a resource management game I designed in 2021. The aim of the game was to produce resources and obtain new resources to help grow your farm. The trading system was a bartering system. Players sold some of their resources to “buy” better ones. Download the game prototype by clicking the button below
