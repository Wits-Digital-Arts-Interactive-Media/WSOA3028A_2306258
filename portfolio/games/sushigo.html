SUSHI GO GAME:

I think this game might make you hungry?

Sushi Go! Is an idle clicker game prototype which I worked. The aim of the game is to make as much sushi as you can to be dubbed the ultimate sushi chef. Players click on the chef to generate as much sushi as the can. With the generated sushi amount players can then use their score to purchase item that can double their score and make even more sushi. Do you have what it takes to be the ultimate sushi chef??
