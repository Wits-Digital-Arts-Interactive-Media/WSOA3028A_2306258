Feedback

So looking back at all the feedback I have gathered from my rubrics and tutors and my users, the people who are my test dummies and test all my work for me, I’m on the right track. I completely understand this because when the users were testing my side, some could navigate through the design journal until I told them to hover over it. This helped me realise that sometimes telling the user what to do isn’t bad, just don’t over-do it.
